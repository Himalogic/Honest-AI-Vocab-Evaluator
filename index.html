Honest AI Vocabulary Evaluator

The AI discussion has become confused by terms that import more meaning than is warranted. Considering the gravity of this moment in human civilization, those writing and speaking about the ramifications of AI ought to consider carefully, and deliberately choose the words they use to refer to AI, its actions, its attributes, and its capabilities. This glossary helps practitioners, philosophers, and prose writers choose terms that describe these systems more precisely, fairly, and honestly.

Inspired by We Are Using the Wrong Words for AI by Dr Sam Illingworth and The Strategic Linguist, with additional entries. This project is open-source and invites serious contributions to this repository by careful taxonomers. Maintained by Justin Philip Flores for Logos Analog.
